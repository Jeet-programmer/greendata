Suitable crops

Corn

Corn is a cereal crop that is widely grown around the world for its edible seeds. It is a staple food in many cultures and can be eaten fresh, canned, or dried. Corn is also used in many industrial products such as biofuels and plastics.

Suitable crops

Rice

Rice is a staple food for many people around the world, particularly in Asia. It is a versatile grain that can be cooked in a variety of ways and used in a wide range of dishes.Rice provides carbohydrates, fiber, and other essential nutrients, making it an important part of a balanced diet.

Suitable crops

Wheat

Wheat is a cereal grain that is a staple food in many parts of the world. It is the most widely cultivated crop globally and provides a significant source of calories and protein for human consumption. Wheat is also used in the production of many food products, such as bread, pasta, and cereal.
